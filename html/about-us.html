Our Story

When the future founder of Wave Shop was just a kid, his world revolved around a small green turtle named Milo. Milo wasn’t just a pet — he was a quiet companion, a steady presence, and the first living thing he ever felt responsible for. One summer afternoon, while exploring the shoreline near his home, he found Milo struggling in the shallows. A piece of plastic packaging had wrapped itself around the turtle’s shell and neck. By the time he freed him, it was too late. Milo didn’t survive. For a child, it was devastating. For the adult he would become, it was defining. He carried that moment with him — the guilt, the anger, the realization that careless human choices could ripple outward and harm the innocent. And he made himself a promise: if he ever built something of his own, it would be part of the solution, not the problem. Years later, that promise became Wave Shop. A store built on responsibility. A business shaped by compassion. A mission born from a single small life that should have had more time. Wave Shop exists because one boy lost his turtle — and decided the world deserved better.
